SGPA Calculator

SGPA (Semester Grade Point Average) measures your performance in a single semester. It’s calculated from the grades and credit hours of all courses taken that semester.

In India, grades are often on a 10-point scale (e.g. O=10, A=9, B=8, etc.), and each subject has a credit value reflecting its weight.

SGPA Formula

>SGPA = (Σ (Credit Points × Grade Points)) ÷ (Σ Credit Points)

Here, Credit Points are the credit hours of each course, and Grade Points are the numeric value of the letter grade for that course.

Explanation of Formula Terms

Credit

Weight of the subject

Grade Point

Numeric value of grade

Total Credits

Sum of all subject credits.

How to Calculate SGPA

1

List Subjects

Note each subject’s credit hours and grade point (from your grade).

2

Multiply

For each subject, multiply its credit hours by its grade point.

3

Sum

Add up all the products to get total weighted grade points.

4

Divide

Divide the total weighted grade points by the total credits.

CGPA Calculator

CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) measures your overall academic performance across multiple semesters. It’s calculated by summing up the SGPA of all semesters and dividing by the total number of semesters.

In India, grades are often on a 10-point scale (e.g. O=10, A=9, B=8, etc.), and each subject has a credit value reflecting its credit value reflecting its weight.

CGPA Formula

>CGPA = (Σ (Credit Points × Grade Points)) ÷ (Σ Credit Points)

Some universities simply average all semester SGPAs when each semester has equal credits.

How to Calculate CGPA

1

Get SGPAs

Use the SGPA formula (or calculator) to find the SGPA for each semester of study.

2

Multiply by Credits

Multiply each semester’s SGPA by the total credit hours of that semester.

3

Sum Up

Add all the results from step 2 to get total weighted points, and also sum all semester credits.

4

Divide

Divide the total weighted points by total credits. The result is your CGPA.

SGPA to Percentage Calculator

This tool transforms a Semester GPA into an approximate percentage score. Indian universities and boards often require percentages for things like admissions, and a common conversion is needed.

SGPA to Percentage Formula

>Percentage = (SGPA – 0.75) × 10

A widely used conversion. But different institutions may use slightly different formulas

Note

Different institutions may use slightly different formulas (some use SGPA×10, others (SGPA−0.75)×10), but both yield similar results. Always check your university’s official conversion if precision is critical

How to Calculate SGPA to Percentage

Compute SGPA

2

Apply Formula

Multiply the SGPA by 10, then subtract 7.5 (which is the same as subtracting 0.75 before multiplying).

3

Result

CGPA to Percentage Formula

>Percentage = CGPA × 9.5

This common rule of thumb (CGPA×9.5) is widely accepted for 10-point grading scales.

This tool does the reverse of SGPA-to-percentage: it finds the SGPA corresponding to a given percentage. Use this if you have a percentage and want an equivalent SGPA on a 10-point scale.

SGPA to Percentage Formula

>SGPA = (Percentage ÷ 10) + 7.5

This particular formula corresponds to the one above in reverse.

How to Calculate SGPA to Percentage

1

Enter Percentage

Start with your percentage score (for one semester).

2

Apply Formula

3

Result
